Learning from Technology

VIT Bhopal University introduces Technology enabled peer learniing, through interactive demonstrations, Projects, case studies and debates. Cources are innovatively designed to incorporate latest developments in academic researches and industrial practices to broaden employment prospects.




Studio Classrooms Integrated Lecture

VIT Bhopal University launches the first integrated ‘Lecture and Laboratory’ learning platform in the country, through novel studio based classrooms that have no black boards and back benches.




Interactive Environment

Interactive learning that enhances theoretical understanding and stimulates out-of-the-box thinking through extensive brain storming sessions, debates and discussions.




Collaborative Team Work

Novel collaborative classroom activities and team work, to build models, design algorithms, create prototypes, solve problems, analyse data, and share knowledge for the development of inter- personal skills.




Swachhn Bharat

Mandatory course credits for Swachh Bharat initiatives have been integrated in the curriculum. The students take pride in being located between the two cleanest cities in the country. This 'Clean India' drive will inspire them to take responsibility in keeping the environment clean.




Value Added Practical Knowledge

Practical Experience augments Theoretical perceptions and concepts, besides re-inteacting the fundamental aspects of the course, providing value added skills that inspires rhe students to explore new and innovative applications.
